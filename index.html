Short Bios

I am a full professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of SUSTech. Before joining SUSTech in 2021, I was an associate professor at Department of Computer Science and Engineering of The Ohio State University, where I started my academic career in 2015 and got promoted in 2019. I received my Ph.D. degree from University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill from Prof. Michael K. Reiter's group in 2014; I obtained my master's and bachelor's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2009 and 2006, respectively.

Research

I am interested in the security of computer systems and networks. While I also work on many other projects, my current research focus is the security and application of trusted execution environments (TEE), which broadly involves research on hardware vulnerabilities, side channels, program analysis, formal verification, crypto protocol design and analysis, blockchain and decentralized computing, machine learning, etc.
